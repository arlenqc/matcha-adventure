Our Story

Rooted in Tradition

A journey from the mist-covered hills of Uji to your morning cup — driven by reverence, refined by nature.

The Beginning

Where It All Started

In the spring of 2018, our founder spent three months living with a tea farming family in Uji, Kyoto Prefecture. She woke before dawn to help cover the tea plants with traditional kanreisha shading, learned the patient art of stone-grinding, and experienced firsthand the meditative stillness that surrounds authentic matcha production.

That experience changed everything. She returned home determined to build a bridge between those quiet hills and the rest of the world — not as a middleman, but as a storyteller, a custodian of craft, and a companion on the path to a more intentional way of living.

Adventure Matcha launched with a single tin of ceremonial grade powder and a conviction: that the simple act of preparing matcha can become a portal to presence, to calm, to something genuinely sacred in an otherwise hurried world.

Philosophy

"Matcha is not a product.
It is a practice."

Our Principles

What Guides Us

01

Transparency

Every product is traceable to its origin farm, harvest date, and processing method. We have nothing to hide because we have everything to be proud of.

02

Purity

No additives, no artificial coloring, no fillers. Just shade-grown, stone-ground tencha leaf — the way matcha was meant to be experienced.

03

Patience

Great matcha cannot be rushed. From the 30-day shading period to the slow stone-grinding process, we honor the timelines that nature demands.

04

Community

We are building more than a store. We are cultivating a global community of people who believe that slowing down is a radical and beautiful act.

Our Journey

Milestones

2018

The Seed Is Planted

Three months in Uji, living with a tea farming family, learning the ancient craft from the ground up. The idea for Adventure Matcha takes root.

2019

First Harvest, First Sale

We partnered with the Horii family in Uji and launched with a single ceremonial grade tin. Sold out in eleven days from a kitchen table in Portland.

2021

Expanding the Ritual

Introduced our Chasen and Chawan collections, partnering with master artisans in Takayama and Kyoto. The full ritual — not just the powder — became our offering.

2023

A Community of 8,000

Reached 8,000 customers across 28 countries. Launched seasonal limited editions and guided virtual tasting experiences with our farm partners.

2025

The Adventure Continues

Twelve farm partners, forty products, and one unwavering mission: to make the transformative power of authentic matcha accessible to everyone willing to slow down.
