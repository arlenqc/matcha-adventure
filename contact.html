Frequently Asked

Keep your matcha sealed in its original tin, stored in a cool, dark place away from heat, light, and moisture. The refrigerator is ideal — just let the tin come to room temperature before opening to prevent condensation. Properly stored, ceremonial matcha stays fresh for about 6 months after opening.

Ceremonial grade is made from the youngest, most tender tea leaves, stone-ground to an ultra-fine powder. It is designed to be whisked with water alone and drunk straight. Culinary grade uses slightly older leaves with a bolder flavor, making it ideal for lattes, smoothies, and baking where the matcha blends with other ingredients.
